    The Starry Night (1889) is an iconic oil painting by Vincent van Gogh.     Girl with a Pearl Earring (c.1665) is a celebrated work by Johannes Vermeer.     About the Painting

    Café Terrace at Night (1888) is a famous painting by Vincent van Gogh. It depicts a brightly lit café in Arles, France, under a starlit sky. The warm glow of the café contrasts with the deep blues of the night, showing Van Gogh’s love for color and atmosphere. Today, it is part of the collection at the Kröller-Müller Museum, Netherlands.     Starry Night Over the Rhône (1888) is a beautiful night scene painted by Vincent van Gogh in Arles, France.
